About Clubs

​Codered​

CodeRed, the official competitive programming club of National Institute of Technology Rourkela, is an endeavour in the direction of refining the problem solving flair. We are a team of driven programmers who are intrigued by the wonder of competitive coding and take strides to foster the culture of the same. We believe in community building and enrich the same through the medium of workshops, contests, discussions etc. Ever since we started, we have  witnessed and cultivated growth in our community in this domain like never before.

​Neuromancers

Neuromancers, the programming society of IIT Bhubaneswar, is a group of some of the most dedicated and talented programmers from the institute. Having a competitive evaluation and selection procedure, only a handful of the top candidates get inducted into the society. The society has members proficient in a plethora of fields, including but not limited to Cloud Computing, Android Development, Machine Learning, Web Development, and are dedicated to organising programming contests and workshops, and make coding a fun experience for everyone.

Any Querie​s ?

​Who can participate?

The competition is open to everyone. Anyone can register for both the events regardless of whether they belong to either of the colleges or not. The registration has to be done separately for both events.

For the team event, the members of any particular team must belong to the same academic year and same college.

​Why should I participate?

If just competing among great minds doesn't excite you, we have prizes and swags worth 1.8 Lakhs waiting just for you.  And of course some other surprises as well for making your experience much better.

​What is the timing of the event?

The timing will be notified before the event day.

​Can I participate individually and in a group too?

Yes, you can participate in both the categories. The group can be of a maximum of 2 students. You must register for each category separately.

​ ​Do I need to pay any registration fee?

No, you don't need to pay a registration fee or any fee to enter this event. If anyone is asking for any payment, then please get in touch with us.
